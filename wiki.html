Overview

AI-powered family chore management app with voice-first onboarding, house scanning, gamified rewards, jobs board, and household routine management. Designed to reduce parental mental load by automating chore scheduling and making chores fun for kids through points, badges, and a rewards shop.

Features

🎤

Voice-First Onboarding

ChatGPT-style conversational setup with speech recognition, TTS, house scanning via GPT-4o Vision, and rewards selection.

🤖

AI Chore Distribution

GPT-4o-mini creates fair 7-day schedules. No same-day duplicates, no consecutive repeats, age-appropriate filtering, fair rotation.

🏆

Gamification

Points (Rookie/Pro/Legend), weekly badges, streak tracking, Weekly Superstar (silver), Monthly Hero (gold), family leaderboard.

🛒

Rewards Shop

Galaxy-themed store where kids buy rewards with points. Parents manage inventory. Per-child reward assignment.

💼

Jobs Board

Parents post jobs (points or cash). Open or application-based. Bidding system. Credibility penalties for overdue jobs.

📋

Family Rules

Bin rotation schedule, pet care (dog walks, cat litter), gaming schedule per child. Visible to entire family.

🔄

Transfer & Support

Transfer chores to siblings (100% points) or ask for help (50/50 split). Smart point distribution on approval.

📷

Smart Room Scanning

Detects robot vacuums, dishwashers, dryers. Suggests maintenance tasks. Scan additional rooms anytime from Settings.

🐾

Auto Pet & Bin Chores

Automatically creates walk, feed, litter, and bin chores from family config during redistribution.

📱

Screen Time Management

Daily limits, chore-gated access, minimum points threshold per child.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Management

Parent/child accounts, invite links (7-day expiry), parent chore participation opt-in.

⭐

Extra Chores

Children can claim bonus chores from other days for extra points. Star burst animation on completion.
